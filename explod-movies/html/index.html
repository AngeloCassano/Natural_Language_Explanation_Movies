MOVIES EXPLANATION

Welcome!

In this experiment you will receive some recommendations about MOVIES you may like and you will be asked to evaluate
the effectiveness of several techniques to EXPLAIN why you have received a certain recommendation.
In the following, we will ask you some information about the items you like.
Next you will receive a recommendation and three types of explanation, and you will be asked
to evaluate the goodness of the recommendation as well as of the explanations you will receive.
The whole experiment will take less than five minutes.
